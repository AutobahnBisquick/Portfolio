About me!

Hi! I'm Connor Sensenig!

My video production journey began making home videos with my friends, but my education truly began in high school. Working on the school news, I discovered a passion that would continue to grow.

I continued developing my skills at Elizabethtown College, starting Jaypardy, Etown College's favorite game show based on nothing at all. Through course work and extracurriculars, I have honed my skills and picked up even more.

I've worked in every position on both sides of the camera, learning how to use hardware, software, and my own ethos

Now I'm looking to continue using these skills in my career. Please feel free to peruse my resume, portfolio, and contact information.
