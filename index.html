About the reel!

This reel shows off some of my favorite work up to 2024! Primarily an editing reel, it puts together some of my best work. Read on to learn about the sources of the footage.

End of Animepardy

The first featured video, and the content that makes the theme of the reel is the intro to Animepardy, a special episode of a gameshow I ran at Elizabethtown College.

The video is a play on the opening credit of the anime Neon Genesis Evangelion. Making personalized versions of the video is a time-honored tradition in the anime community, so it seemed appropriate for the special.

Planning for the video meant creating a storyboard of the Evangelion opening, as well as a shot sheet in order efficiently move through production. Recording footage took place in a studio in front of a greenscreen according to the shot sheet.

Editing and effects took place in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro.
